Hook Head Lighthouse

Discover Ireland's Oldest Operational Lighthouse

Guided Lighthouse Tours

Join our expert guides as they take you on a journey through centuries of maritime history. Learn about the fascinating past of Hook Head Lighthouse and its vital role in guiding sailors safely to shore.

With spectacular coastal views and an opportunity to step inside this historic tower, our guided tours offer an unforgettable experience for visitors of all ages.

Coastal Walking Trails

Explore the breathtaking landscapes surrounding Hook Head with our scenic coastal walking trails. Enjoy the fresh sea breeze and witness stunning views of the rugged Irish coastline.

These trails are perfect for nature lovers, photographers, and anyone seeking a tranquil escape into the beauty of Ireland’s southeastern shores.

Café and Visitor Centre

Relax and unwind at our cozy café, offering delicious locally sourced food and refreshing beverages. Whether you're looking for a light snack or a hearty meal, we have something for everyone.

Visit our interactive Visitor Centre to learn more about the lighthouse's history, marine life, and local legends. A great spot for families and history enthusiasts alike!
